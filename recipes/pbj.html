Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A PB & J is a great sandwich that contains the key ingredients of peanut butter, and, you guessed it! Jelly!

Very delicious!

Ingredients

  • bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. Step 1 Grab two slices of bread
  2. Step 2 Lather one of the slices with peanut butter on one side
  3. Step 3 Lather the other slice with jelly on one side
  4. Step 4 Places the two slices of bread together so that the lathered sides are touching
